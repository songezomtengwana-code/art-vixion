About Us
Art Vixion: A small NGO with a big passion for music and videography. We are a dedicated music & video publishing company that specializes in administering audio and audiovisual content. Our mission is to support talented artists and creators by helping them protect their musical works, distribute their content, and ensure they receive fair compensation for their artistry. Through our commitment to fostering harmony in the music industry, we aim to empower artists to share their stories and melodies with the world.
